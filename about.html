Created in 2003, Kinship is a long time staple of the Fresno, CA music scene. Called a band at the "Intersection of the 90's and now", the Band's sound keeps evolving from one song to the next. Attempting to push the limits of their sound, Kinship has influences for all over the map of Alternative, Rock, and gets some influence from Electronic, as well as Folk music of many different cultures.

Kinship is definitively a live band, and the shows run the gauntlet between calm and relaxing to a full hurricane of sound and motion.

Kinship released the EP ALPHAS in 2012. The band has been reviewed multiple times by local papers, had music played and been interviewed on the radio in places like Salt Lake City and Cheyenne, WY.

Local band Kinship has a sound formed at the intersection of the '90s and now.
